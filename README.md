Project Description

SheSkill is a women-focused skill-sharing platform where users can explore skills, connect with mentors, chat, and book sessions. It helps women showcase their talents and learn new skills in a supportive community.

The Problem Statement

Many women lack accessible, safe, and community-driven platforms to share their skills or learn from other women. Existing platforms are often too general and don‚Äôt focus on empowering women or building trusted mentorship spaces.

The Solution

SheSkill provides a dedicated platform for women to:

* Showcase their skills

* Discover mentors

* Chat and connect

* Book learning sessions

*This creates a safe ecosystem for learning, teaching, and networking.
